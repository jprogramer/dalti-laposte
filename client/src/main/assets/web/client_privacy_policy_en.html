Privacy Policy for the "Dalti" service

Last edit date: November 22, 2021

This privacy policy governs the manner in which we collect and use information about all users of the “Dalti” service, including users of the “Dalti-laposte” Android application and visitors to the “dalti-laposte.com” website.

What personal information do we collect?

We never collect personal information automatically, this personal information includes (but is not limited to): username, phone number, email, geographic location, unique serial number of the user's device, etc.

Instead, we directly request such information when needed from the user himself, and even in this case we do not use this information as an identifier for the user, but only within the context in which it was collected.

The above policy imposes some restrictions on the ways of using our services, for example, when a user deletes and reinstalls one of our applications on his device, we will not be able to identify him as a previous user and will be considered a new user, this will cause the user to lose: any ongoing activation, some limited trial periods, some special discounts, etc. Note that this limitation does not apply when updating the app to a newer version.

Why don't we collect personal information automatically?

According to our philosophy, every user of our service trust that our applications are reliable and free from any viruses or spyware, and we consider the automatic collection of personal information as a betrayal to this trust, while requesting this information from the user as an extension to it, in addition to the fact that information collected automatically lacks credibility.

What other information do we collect?

We automatically collect certain (non-personally identifiable) information about Users when they use our Service, including (but not limited to) the type of device used, the time of use, some settings such as the prefered language, etc.

This information helps us improve the quality of our service (for example we may notice that certain types of devices are taking longer than necessary to receive updates), and this information also helps us to customize content to fit the user more (for example we may modify certain content if the user's device has a small screen).

Who do we share the information we collect with?

We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal information to any third party, but only share some general and non-personal information with “Google Play Services” in order to track some general statistics such as the number of downloads from “Google Play Store”, number of users by device type, etc.

Ads and external content

We do not display any paid advertisements or external content on any of our applications (or websites), and any advertisement or offer that a user may encounter is directly affiliated with our Service even if it is not explicitly stated.

Contact us

If you have any questions or suggestions, you can contact us through: contact@dalti-laposte.com
