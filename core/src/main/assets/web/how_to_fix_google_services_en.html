How to fix Google Play services problems:

Last edit date: November 24, 2021

We are sorry that you are having some problems using our application. We are at your service around the clock at: contact@dalti-laposte.com

You can try the following solutions:

General solutions:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Disable any VPN, firewall or similar service.
  • Check that you have added at least one Google account (Gmail) to this device.
  • Update Google Play services to the latest version.
  • Clear the data of Google Play services application, then restart your device.
  • Reinstall this application.

Specific solutions based on your error code:

Error 1: Google Play services not installed.

Solutions:

  • Install Google Play services from Google Play store.
  • Restart your device.
  • Open this application again.

Error 2: Google Play services version is too old.

Solutions:

  • Update Google Play services from Google Play store.
  • Restart your device.
  • Open this application again.

Error 3: Google Play services is disabled.

Solutions:

  • Open Google Play services details in settings.
  • Enable Google Play services.
  • Open this application again.

Error 4 or 6: Google account needed (Gmail).

Solution:

  • Add another Google account (or create a new one) to your device.
  • Open this application again.

Error 19: Google Play services requires more permissions.

Solutions:

  • Open Google Play services details in settings.
  • Check if Google Play services has at least the main permissions (“Phone”, “SMS”, “Call logs”, “Contacts” and “Files and media”).
  • Open this application again.

Error 7: Internet connection error.

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Disable any VPN, firewall or similar service.
  • Check that you have added at least one Google account (Gmail) to this device.
  • Open this application again.

Error 8 or 9: Internal failure in Google Play services.

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Restart your device.
  • Wait a few minutes (for Google services to start and connect).
  • Open this application again.

Error 18: Google Play services is currently being updated on this device.

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Wait for a few minutes.
  • Open this application again.

Error 22: Google Play store error.

Solution:

  • Ensure that you have added at least one Google account (Gmail) to this device.
  • Clear Google Play store data.
  • Update Google Play services application.
  • Restart your device.
  • Open this application again.

Error 23 or 24: A needed Google service is disabled for this application.

Solution:

  • Ensure that the Google Play services application is not disabled.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Restart your device and wait a few minutes (for Google services to start and connect).
  • Open this application again.

Error 5, 16 or 20: A needed service is not available on this device or for the current Google account. Updating Google Play services will not likely solve the problem.

Solution:

  • Remove your current Google account (Gmail) from this device.
  • Restart your device.
  • Add another Google account (or create a new one).
  • Wait a few minutes (for Google services to connect).
  • Open this application again.

Error 1500: SD card not found, this will happen only on an old Google Play services version because new versions don't require an SD card.

Solution:

  • Update Google Play services application.
  • Restart your device.
  • Open this application again.

or

  • Turn your device off.
  • Mount an SD card.
  • Turn your device on.
  • Ensure that the SD card is working and doesn’t need formatting.
  • Open this application again.

Other Errors:

If you are facing an error that is not listed in the previews list then we highly recommend that you contact us at: contact@dalti-laposte.com so we can help you fix it as soon as possible!
